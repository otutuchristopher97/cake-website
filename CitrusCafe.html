About Us

We can hold events of all sizes in our dedicated Britannia Suite. Facilities include whiteboard, projector, laptop points and free-flowing tea and coffee!

Our restaurant was purpose-built in 2002 but with all the open fires, secluded dining areas and delicious pub food favourites of a country pub restaurant, we offer a warm welcome to guests who can enjoy al fresco dining on the patio and attractive gardens in summer or relax by a cosy fire in winter.
